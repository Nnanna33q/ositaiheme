Osita Iheme

We all know he's one of the best Nigerian actors Nollywood produced. Osita Iheme (Born on 20th February 1982 in Imo State) is an actor, author and producer.
He is popularly known as Pawpaw in the film Aki na Uwa. Despite his small physique, his small body is filled with charisma. He brought many families
together in their living rooms and put a big smile on our faces in the early 2000s. Looking back on those days gives me undeniable feeling of nostalgia. I am grateful for Osita Iheme's contributions to Nollywood and to the world. He is a talented actor, a kind and compassionate person and a true inspiration. I wish him all the best in the years to come.

Achievements

  1. He was awarded the Nigerian National Honor Of Member of the Order of The Federal Replublic by Goodluck Jonathan in 2011.



  2. In 2007, he was given the life time Achievement award by AMAA (African Movie Academy Awards).



  3. He wrote a motivational book titled Inspired 101.The main purpose of the book is to inspire, motivate and stimulate the minds of young Nigerians
